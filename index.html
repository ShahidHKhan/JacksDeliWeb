About Us

Located in the heart of New Paltz, Jack’s Deli serves up sizzling gyros, flavorful halal carts, hearty chopped cheeses, and mouth-watering hot & cold sandwiches.

We also carry fresh deli meats and a wide selection of exotic snacks & drinks. Proudly supplied by Boar’s Head.
